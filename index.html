About

Profile

Anushka Saxena is a Staff Research Analyst with Takshashila's Geostrategy Programme. She is also a Doctoral Scholar at the Manipal Academy of Higher Education's Department of Geopolitics and International Relations. Her research focuses on the Chinese People's Liberation Army and its reforms, China-Taiwan relations, and India's foreign policy. She curates the weekly newsletter Eye on China.

She has previously worked with think tanks such as the Observer Research Foundation, Institute of Chinese Studies, and Centre for Social and Economic Progress, and has written for platforms such as MP-IDSA, Centre for Land Warfare Studies, and Vivekananda International Foundation. She obtained her Master's in International Relations from O.P. Jindal Global University (2023) and her B.A. (Hons) in History from Lady Shri Ram College for Women, University of Delhi (2021).
